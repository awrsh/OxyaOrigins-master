Oxya Origin represents a collection of 7989 Oxyans from 3 Planets, representing 9 unique Tribes. Our artistic team handrawn more than 250 attributes and various level of rarity. Oxyans are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS.


We are, in the blockchain, the 1st collaborate-to-earn RPG allowing land conquests, social experiments, in-game governance.

A vast ecosystem with innovative utility, community building, and epic adventure for all players (developed over the months) built around talented artists, game developers studio, experienced entrepreneurs, stuffed marketing team.

EARLY CYCLE

LUXION

Niefelheim

These people have been enhanced by cybernetic & mechanical prostheses, allowing them to flirt with immortality.


They’re run by an IA that made every individual nothing more than a soldier serving the cause of Artificial Intelligence. Humans killed in battle are recycled and reassembled to forge new combatants.

After a few cycles, these people don’t have any human parts left, becoming robotized entities.
